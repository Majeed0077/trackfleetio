KG-22 Specification
KG-22 is a rugged 4G vehicle tracker, which is compact and
waterproof, designed for harsh environments. It features

positioning for optimal tracking efficiency.

1. 4G Cat.1
2. GNSS: GPS/ GALILEO/ BeiDou/ GLONASS
